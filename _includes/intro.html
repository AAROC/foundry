What is cloud foundry ?

Cloud Foundry is an open, informal, unopinionated, collaborative workshop for e-Infrastructure Engineers to relate their experience in building the next platform for e-Science.

Is this for me?

Are you in a support, development or engineering role ? Do you care about the sustainability of research platforms ? Are you grappling with how to support large or diverse research communities ? Are your users pushing for elastic, novel, or on-demand resources ? Are you ecstatic about new container and cloud platforms; or do you feel a nagging skepticism about them ?
